ABOUT SURYODAY

2010 Established Year
15+ Years Excellence

Established in the year 2010, Suryoday Engineers & Control Systems has built an acclaimed reputation as a leading solution provider of specialized Electrical, Automation, and Instrumentation engineering services.

We deliver an extensive gamut of highly technical industrial solutions, ranging from the in-house custom designing and wiring of intricate electrical panels to full-scale engineering commissioning of field instruments, and specialized PLC programming across top-tier international brands.
